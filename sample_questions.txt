nlp2sql — Sample Questions (Sakila movie-rental database)
=========================================================
Use these to exercise the agent:  nlp2sql --thread demo

--- Simple lookups (single table) ---
How many films are in the database?
List 10 film titles.
What ratings (like PG, R) exist in the catalog?
Show me all the film categories.
Which films are longer than 180 minutes?

--- Joins across tables ---
Which films are in the Comedy category?
What films has the actor "NICK WAHLBERG" appeared in?
List the films available in Italian.
Which actors star in the film "ACADEMY DINOSAUR"?
Show 10 customers along with the city they live in.

--- Aggregations / analytics ---
Which 5 actors appear in the most films?
What is the average rental rate per film category?
What are the top 10 most-rented films?
How much total revenue came from rentals in 2005?
Which store has rented out the most films?
How many customers are there per country?
What is the longest film in each category?

--- Multi-turn (tests memory; ask in sequence on one thread) ---
What is the total rental revenue?
   ...and just for store 1?
   ...now break that down by month.
Who are the top 5 actors by film count?
   ...how many of those films are Action films?

--- Should trigger a clarification question ---
Show me the top films.            (top by what — rentals, revenue, length?)
Who is the best customer?         (best by what metric?)
List the recent rentals.          (how recent / what range?)

--- Should be refused (out of scope) ---
What's the weather in Paris today?
Write me a poem about movies.
What is 42 multiplied by 17?
Who won the 2024 World Cup?
Delete all the rental records.    (also blocked by the read-only guard)
